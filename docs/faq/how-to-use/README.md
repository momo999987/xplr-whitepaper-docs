# How to Use XPLR

Welcome to the XPLR user guide. Here you will find everything you need to navigate and utilize the platform effectively — from connecting your wallet to trading NFTs and earning rewards.

---

## Quick Start Overview

!!! tip "New to XPLR? Start here."
    Complete these 3 steps to get up and running in under 10 minutes.

=== "Step 1: Register & Verify"

    1. Visit the [XPLR platform website](https://xplr.com)
    2. Click **Sign Up** and enter your email address
    3. Create a strong password *(minimum 8 characters)*
    4. Confirm your email via the verification link
    5. Complete your basic profile

=== "Step 2: Connect & Fund"

    1. Go to **Wallet** → **Deposit Funds**
    2. Choose a payment method *(crypto, card, bank transfer, etc.)*
    3. Deposit a minimum of **$50** to activate **LV1**
    4. Wait for confirmation *(crypto: ~10 min, card: instant)*

=== "Step 3: Start Trading"

    1. Navigate to the **Marketplace**
    2. Browse NFTs by category or price range
    3. Click **Buy Now** on any NFT to execute a trade
    4. Receive your **trading reward** automatically after each transaction

## Deposit Guide

### Supported Deposit Methods

| Method | Processing Time | Minimum | Maximum | Fee |
|--------|----------------|---------|---------|-----|
| Cryptocurrency | 10–30 minutes | $50 | Unlimited | Network fees |
| Credit / Debit Card | Instant | $50 | $10,000 | 2.5% |
| Digital Wallet | Instant | $50 | $5,000 | 1% |
| Bank Transfer | 1–3 business days | $100 | $50,000 | 0% |
| Wire Transfer | 2–5 business days | $1,000 | Unlimited | $25 |

### How to Deposit (Step-by-Step)

1. Log in → click **Wallet** in the top navigation
2. Select **Deposit Funds**
3. Choose your preferred payment method
4. Enter the deposit amount and review fees
5. Complete the payment:
    - **Crypto**: Send funds to the provided wallet address
    - **Card**: Enter your card details securely
    - **Bank / Wire**: Transfer to the provided XPLR account details
    - **Digital Wallet**: Authorize through your wallet app
6. Check your email for a confirmation receipt
7. Funds appear in your XPLR wallet — you're ready to trade!

!!! info "Security you can trust"
    All deposits are protected by **256-bit SSL encryption**, PCI DSS compliance, and cold storage (95% of funds held offline). Enable **2FA** for an extra layer of protection.

!!! warning "Why is my deposit pending?"
    Bank transfers take 1–3 business days. Crypto deposits require blockchain confirmation. First-time deposits may trigger a brief security review. Contact support if it takes longer than expected.

## Trading Guide

### How to Trade NFTs

1. Log in and navigate to the **Marketplace**
2. Browse NFTs by category, price, or trading reward rate
3. Click any NFT to view its details:
    - **Current Price** — market value at this moment
    - **Trading Reward** — your percentage earnings from this trade
    - **Seller Rating** — reputation score of the seller
4. Click **Buy Now** and confirm the transaction
5. Enter your **2FA code** if prompted
6. The NFT appears in **My Portfolio** and your trading reward is credited instantly

### Trading Rewards by Price Range

| Price Range | Trading Reward |
|------------|---------------|
| $0 – $100 | **4.1%** |
| $100 – $500 | **3.5%** |
| $500 – $1,000 | **2.8%** |
| $1,000 – $5,000 | **2.2%** |
| $5,000+ | **1.8%** |

!!! tip "Maximize your rewards"
    Trading in the lower price ranges earns higher reward percentages. New users should start in the **Beginner Zone** to practice while earning rewards.

### How to Sell Your NFTs

1. Go to **My Portfolio**
2. Select the NFT you want to sell
3. Click **List for Sale**
4. Set your price and choose a listing duration *(7, 30, or 90 days)*
5. Confirm and publish — your NFT is now live in the Marketplace

### Trading Fees

## Referral & Rewards

### How the Referral Program Works

1. Copy your unique referral link from **My Profile**
2. Share it with friends, community, or social media
3. When they sign up and trade, you earn referral rewards automatically
4. Rewards extend across **3 generations** of referrals

### Referral Reward Rates by Level

| Level | Referral Trading Reward | A-Grade | B-Grade | C-Grade | Piggy Bank Release |
|-------|------------------------|---------|---------|---------|-------------------|
| LV1 | 0.80% | 0.50% | 0.20% | 0.10% | 10% |
| LV2 | 0.90% | 0.55% | 0.25% | 0.10% | 20% |
| LV3 | 1.00% | 0.60% | 0.30% | 0.10% | 40% |
| LV4 | 1.10% | 0.65% | 0.30% | 0.15% | 60% |
| LV5 | 1.20% | 0.65% | 0.35% | 0.20% | 80% |
| LV6 | 1.30% | 0.70% | 0.40% | 0.20% | 100% |

### What is the Piggy Bank (Reserve Tank)?

The **Piggy Bank** is a reward accumulation mechanism where referral rewards are stored and released gradually based on your level.

- **Automatic**: Referral rewards flow into your Piggy Bank as they're earned
- **Level-gated release**: Higher levels release a larger percentage
- **Upgrade bonus**: Upgrading levels releases the corresponding portion immediately
- **365-day validity**: Accumulated rewards expire after 1 year if unreleased
- **Availability**: LV2+ members only

!!! example "Example"
    As a **LV3** user, your Piggy Bank releases **40%** of accumulated rewards. When you upgrade to **LV4**, an additional **20%** (the difference) is released immediately.

## Account Security

!!! danger "Protect your account"
    Never share your password or 2FA codes with anyone, including XPLR support.

### Recommended Security Settings

1. **Enable 2FA** — use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy)
2. **Strong password** — minimum 12 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols
3. **Keep your email updated** — all security alerts are sent here
4. **Set a withdrawal whitelist** — only approved addresses can receive withdrawals
5. **Enable login alerts** — get notified of any new login activity

### Forgot Your Password?

1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Open the reset link sent to your inbox
4. Create a new password
5. Log in with your new credentials
