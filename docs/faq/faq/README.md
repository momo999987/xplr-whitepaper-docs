# FAQ

## Platform Basics

**Q: What is XPLR?**
A: XPLR is the world's first comprehensive NFT trading platform based on algorithmic trading. Our platform combines innovative trading mechanisms with community-driven incentives to create a sustainable ecosystem for NFT enthusiasts.

**Q: How does the Pooling Algorithm Model work?**
A: It aggregates all NFTs in a pool where buyers and sellers interact directly, ensuring instant settlement and continuous liquidity. This model eliminates traditional order matching delays and provides fair pricing for all participants.

**Q: How do I earn rewards?**
A: You can earn trading rewards (1.8% to 4.1% per transaction depending on price range) and referral rewards from your team's activities. Your earning potential increases as you level up within the platform.

## Trading & Transactions

**Q: How do I deposit funds into XPLR?**
A: You can deposit funds through multiple channels including bank transfers, cryptocurrency transfers, and digital payment methods. Simply navigate to your wallet section and select your preferred deposit method. Deposits are processed instantly for most payment methods.

**Q: How do I withdraw my earnings?**
A: Withdrawals can be initiated from your account dashboard. Select your preferred withdrawal method (bank transfer, cryptocurrency, or other options), enter the amount, and confirm. Processing times vary by method but typically complete within 1-3 business days.

**Q: How do I trade on XPLR?**
A: Browse the NFT marketplace, select an asset you wish to trade, review the current price and trading rewards, and execute the transaction. Your trading rewards are automatically credited to your account and can be reinvested or withdrawn.

**Q: What are the trading fees?**
A: XPLR charges minimal transaction fees while rewarding traders with 1.8%-4.1% in trading rewards depending on the price range. This means you earn while you trade, creating a unique value proposition.

**Q: What is the Piggy Bank (Reserve Tank) mechanism?**
A: The Piggy Bank is a savings mechanism that accumulates referral rewards. As you level up, your Piggy Bank rewards decrease but your direct trading rewards increase. Your Piggy Bank balance is released according to your level's release ratio each time you upgrade. Piggy Bank rewards have a 365-day validity period and are recalculated after expiration.

**Q: How do team levels work?**
A: Your referral network is organized into three generations:
- **First Generation**: Users you directly invite
- **Second Generation**: Users invited by your first-generation referrals
- **Third Generation**: Users invited by your second-generation referrals

You earn referral rewards from all three generations based on your user level.

**Q: What are the requirements to reach higher levels?**
A: To unlock higher levels and access premium features like the Reserve Tank, you need to meet specific conditions including:
- Maintaining active status as a platform participant
- Building a qualified referral network with minimum requirements for A, B, and C-grade referrals
- Meeting tenure requirements (typically 1 year for Reserve Tank access)

## Account & Levels

**Q: What are user levels?**
A: XPLR features a 6-tier user level system (LV1-LV6) that determines your earning potential. Higher levels unlock better reward rates, exclusive features, and access to premium mechanisms like the Reserve Tank.

**Q: How do I increase my user level?**
A: User levels are determined by your activity, trading volume, and referral network performance. As you trade more and build a stronger team, you'll automatically progress to higher levels with better rewards.

**Q: What is the Reserve Tank?**
A: The Reserve Tank is an exclusive mechanism for high-level users (typically LV6) that provides additional commission income. It requires meeting specific conditions including 1 year of active participation and maintaining a qualified referral network.
